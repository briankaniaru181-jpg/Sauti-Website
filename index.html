Our Mission

Umilisi AI was born from a fundamental truth: Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum is delivered almost entirely in English yet for millions of students, English is not a first language.

We build AI tools that meet learners where they are. Our flagship product, CBC Msaidizi, lets students query the full CBC syllabus in Swahili for the first time.

At its core, Umilisi signifies more than just knowledge — it represents competence. We build AI tools that ensure every Kenyan learner can achieve this level of mastery in their own tongue.
